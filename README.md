# Santa Cruz County Civil Grand Jury Data

## Description
This is a summary of all Santa Cruz County Civil Grand Jury reports since the 2015-2016 Civil Grand Jury, exlcuding Honoring Commitments reports. All reports were categorized by hand. There might be mistakes. All reports were retrieved from the [Santa Cruz County Civil Grand Jury website](https://www.santacruzcountyca.gov/Departments/GrandJury.aspx#report).

## Methodology
Every report from the Civil Grand Jury (exlcuding Honoring Commitments) was added into a CSV with the year, Entity or Entities, Category or Categories and Location. The Entity and Category descriptions were created by me and limited to two entries for each for practical reasons. If a report has more than two entities or could conceivably fall into more than two categories, I chose what I felt fit best and excluded any excess. The raw CSV is in the repository and I encourage anyone interested to look at and modify the CSV if desired.
